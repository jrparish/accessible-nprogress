Accessible NProgress.js

A nanoscopic progress bar. With realistic trickle animations to tell your users that something's happening!

NProgress.start() — shows the progress bar

NProgress.set(0.4) — sets a percentage

NProgress.inc() — increments by a little

NProgress.done() — completes the progress

NProgress.promise(promise) — Progress completes when promise(s) is resolved - additive (try clicking the button multiple times)
Demo uses promises that are resolved randomly between 0 and 3 seconds (doesn't work in IE 11 without a polyfill)

Perfect for jQuery, Axios, Turbolinks, Pjax, and other Ajax-heavy apps.
